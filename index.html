Property Check-Ins

Light Property Management Support

Standard visit

  • Exterior walk-around for visible issues such as damage, standing water, and entry points
  • Interior walkthrough for obvious leaks, moisture, odors, or unexpected changes
  • Confirm heat and basic power status and check key settings the owner specifies
  • Quick internet check when possible
  • Photo report after each visit with notes and recommended next steps

How to get started

  • Book onboarding and confirm visit frequency
  • Share access method (lockbox or smart lock) and emergency contacts
  • Receive a simple checklist and reporting schedule

Important notes

Visits are visual check-ins and reporting. This is not a security service and not a guarantee against loss. Final scope can vary by property type and season. Travel fees may apply outside the standard service radius.

Optional add-ons

  • Seasonal readiness checks (winterization and spring start-up items)
  • Basic smart home health check during visits (Wi-Fi, thermostats, cameras if installed)
  • Vendor coordination notes (share findings with your plumber, HVAC, or contractor)

Best for

  • Absentee owners and second homes
  • Insurance-friendly documentation (photos and visit notes)
  • Peace of mind between trips
