Frequently Asked Questions

What brands do you work with?

I work with all major smart home brands including Google Nest, Amazon Alexa, Apple HomeKit, Ring, Ecobee, Philips Hue, TP-Link, Lutron, August, and many others. I'll recommend the best options based on your existing setup and preferences.

Do I need to buy equipment before you come?

No. During our initial chat, I'll recommend specific products based on your needs and what you already own. You can purchase them yourself or I can include procurement in the service.

How long does a typical smart home setup take?

Most single-device installations (thermostat, doorbell, lock) take 1-2 hours. Whole-home setups can take 3-6 hours depending on complexity. I'll give you a time estimate after our initial discussion.

Can you install a smart thermostat if I have an older HVAC system?

In most cases, yes. During our consultation, I'll ask about your current system and determine compatibility. Some older systems may need a C-wire adapter, which I can install as part of the service.

What if something stops working after you leave?

I provide follow-up support to make sure everything continues working smoothly. Most issues can be resolved with a quick phone call or remote assistance.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes! For property management clients and short-term rental hosts, I offer regular tech check-ins as part of property visits. For residential clients, I'm available on-call for tune-ups and troubleshooting.

How far do you travel?

My primary service area is Collingwood, Blue Mountains, and Wasaga Beach. I can travel to nearby communities with a small travel fee. Contact me to confirm your location.

Do you work with renters or only homeowners?

I work with both! For renters, I focus on non-permanent solutions like smart plugs, portable devices, and equipment that can move with you. Always check with your landlord before installing anything that requires wiring or permanent modification.
