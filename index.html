Frequently Asked Questions

What brands do you work with?

I work with all major smart home brands including Google Nest, Amazon Alexa, Apple HomeKit, Ring, Ecobee, Philips Hue, TP-Link, Lutron, August, and many others. I'll recommend the best options based on your existing setup and preferences.

Do I need to buy equipment before you come?

No. During our initial chat, I'll recommend specific products based on your needs and what you already own. You can purchase them yourself or I can include procurement in the service.

How long does a typical smart home setup take?

Most single-device installations (thermostat, doorbell, lock) take 1-2 hours. TV mounting typically takes 1-2 hours. Aging in place setups can take 3-6 hours depending on the number of sensors and devices. I'll give you a time estimate after our initial discussion.

Is aging in place technology complicated to use?

Not at all. Most systems are passive—they work in the background without any daily interaction required. Family members can check in remotely through simple apps, while the person living at home doesn't need to do anything special. I focus on simple, reliable technology that doesn't require tech expertise.

Will monitoring systems invade privacy?

Privacy is a top priority. I use passive sensors that detect activity patterns without cameras in private spaces. The person living at home maintains full control and privacy, while family members receive alerts only for safety concerns (like falls, unusual inactivity, or environmental issues). Everything is transparent and discussed upfront.

Can you install a smart thermostat if I have an older HVAC system?

In most cases, yes. During our consultation, I'll ask about your current system and determine compatibility. Some older systems may need a C-wire adapter, which I can install as part of the service.

Do you provide the TV mount and cables?

I can provide mounts and cables, or you can purchase them yourself. I'll recommend the right mount type based on your TV size and wall type, and ensure all cables are properly concealed for a clean, professional look.

What if something stops working after you leave?

I provide follow-up support to make sure everything continues working smoothly. Most issues can be resolved with a quick phone call or remote assistance.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes! For aging in place clients, I offer regular check-ins and system health monitoring. For short-term rental hosts, I offer regular tech check-ins as part of property visits. For residential clients, I'm available on-call for tune-ups and troubleshooting.

How far do you travel?

My primary service area is Collingwood, Blue Mountains, and Wasaga Beach. I can travel to nearby communities with a small travel fee. Contact me to confirm your location.

Do you work with renters or only homeowners?

I work with both! For renters, I focus on non-permanent solutions like smart plugs, portable devices, and equipment that can move with you. Always check with your landlord before installing anything that requires wiring or permanent modification.
