Keyless Entry That Actually Works

A smart lock is one of the highest-value upgrades you can make to a home or vacation property. Done right, it eliminates keys entirely, gives you full visibility into who comes and goes, and allows trusted people — family members, caregivers, cleaning services, contractors — to enter without you being there. Done wrong, it is a frustrating device that drops offline, fails in cold weather, or never gets properly integrated with the rest of your home.

I install smart locks throughout Collingwood, Blue Mountains, and Wasaga Beach for primary residences, vacation properties, and short-term rentals. Every installation includes hardware assessment, proper fitting to your existing deadbolt hardware, app setup, access code configuration, and a full walkthrough before I leave. You get a lock that works reliably — not one that needs troubleshooting every few weeks.

What Smart Lock Installation Includes

Hardware Assessment

I confirm your door hardware and deadbolt configuration before recommending a lock. Not every smart lock fits every door — backset measurements, door thickness, and existing hardware all factor into the right choice.

Professional Installation

Proper fitting, alignment, and physical testing before any app setup begins. A smart lock that is not mechanically sound will fail regardless of how well the software is configured.

App Setup & Integration

Full app configuration on your phone, integration with your existing smart home platform (Google Home, Amazon Alexa, or Apple HomeKit), and voice assistant pairing where applicable.

Access Code Configuration

Setup of permanent codes for household members, temporary codes for guests or service providers, and scheduled codes that activate and expire automatically on dates you set.

Remote Access

Configuration of remote lock and unlock capability so you can manage your door from anywhere. Includes activity log setup so you receive a timestamped record of every entry.

Automation Integration

Where applicable, integration with your existing routines — automatic locking at a set time each night, unlock triggers tied to arrival, and coordination with your video doorbell or security system.

Smart Locks for Vacation Properties

For properties in Blue Mountains and the Collingwood area that sit vacant between visits or host short-term rental guests, a smart lock is not a convenience — it is an operational necessity. Guest check-in codes can be created and shared remotely before arrival and set to expire automatically at checkout. You never need to coordinate a key handoff, hide a lockbox, or wonder whether a previous guest's key was returned.

Every entry is logged with a timestamp. If a guest reports a problem on arrival, you can confirm remotely whether they actually entered the property. If a cleaning crew arrives late, you know. If someone enters when no one should be there, you are notified immediately.

Smart Locks for Aging in Place

For seniors living independently, a smart lock removes one of the most common daily friction points — fumbling with keys at the door. Keyless entry means no keys to lose, no lockouts, and no reaching awkwardly to find the right key. Family members can unlock remotely if needed, and caregivers receive their own unique codes that can be enabled or revoked at any time without changing the lock itself.
